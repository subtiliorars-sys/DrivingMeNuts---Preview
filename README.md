# Driving Me Nuts 🥜🚚

A pixel-art RPG + idle game about running a food truck that sells roasted
peanuts — and quietly teaches you how to run a real small business.

> Customer: "You know peanuts aren't actually nuts, right? They're legumes."
>
> Owner: "I know, I know — it's driving me nuts!"

## What it is
- **Drive & explore (RPG):** take your truck around town, meet regulars,
  chase permits, scout the best corners.
- **Roast & grow (idle):** queue roasts, set prices, upgrade the rig —
  the truck earns while you're away.
- **Learn for real:** margins, cash flow, permits, location strategy, and
  seasonality are the actual game mechanics, not flavor text.

## Status
