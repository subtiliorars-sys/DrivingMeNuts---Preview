Driving Me Nuts

A peanut is neither a nut nor a legume — it's a small-business simulation disguised as a cozy RPG.

🏪 Run Your Own Peanut Roasting Empire

Roast, price, and sell peanuts with real supply-and-demand economics. Build relationships with quirky NPC customers. Manage cash flow, debt, and growth — learn what COGS means by feeling it.

▶ Play Free Demo 🛒 Wishlist on itch.io 🚜 Coming to Steam

🌰 Roast & Trade

Set your margins too high and watch customers walk; too low and you'll never cover the cart payment. Real economics, no hand-holding.

👥 NPC Relationships

Customers remember how you treated them. Loyal patrons unlock new recipes and upgrades. Betray them at your peril.

📊 Business Skills

Take out loans, pay them back (or don't), and discover why every small business needs a rainy-day fund.

🔒 Privacy First

All data lives in browser local storage. No accounts, no telemetry, no data leaves your machine.

🛠 Built With

Phaser 3 + TypeScript + Vite. Browser-playable, no install required.
